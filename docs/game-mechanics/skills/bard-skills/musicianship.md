# Musicianship

## Overview

Musicianship is required to successfully use instruments and barding skills like Provocation, Discordance and Peacemaking.

## Exceptional instruments

Exceptional instruments can be crafted by a carpenter with enough Musicianship.

## Training

Train from Bard NPCs to reach around 30.

Musicianship doesn't have any difficulty checks, keep playing instruments until you reach 100.

To be more efficient, you can gain Musicianship while training other barding skills.
