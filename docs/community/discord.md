## Discord Rules

### Basic Rules

1. **Be Respectful:** Treat everyone with kindness
2. **No Spam:** Don't flood channels
3. **Stay On Topic:** Use appropriate channels
4. **No Advertising:** Don't promote other servers
5. **Follow Discord ToS:** Abide by Discord rules

### Trading Rules

- Use #trading channel
- Be honest about items
- Complete trades fairly
- Report scammers to staff

## Voice Channels

### Available Rooms

- General voice chat

### Voice Etiquette

- Use push-to-talk or noise gate
- Mute when not talking
- Be respectful
- Keep background noise low

## Getting Help on Discord

### Support Tickets

- Open a support ticket

- Track your issues

### Mentioning Staff

- Use @staff for urgent issues

- Be patient for response
- Provide all relevant info
