## ğŸ¨ Charter Hue

A flaw in the dyeing process previously allowed the Charter Hue to be applied to bolts and large piles of cloth. This was never intended.

In the early New Dawn Era, scholars discovered a rare pigment that carried a faint residual essence from the Codex of Ultimate Wisdom itself. That essence bonds slowly and deliberately with individual garments, giving the Charter Hue its legendary depth. When spread recklessly across bulk cloth, the essence rejects the bond, and it slowly fades.

- All Charter Hue pigment previously applied to bolts and large cloth piles has now faded. Individually dyed garments remain unaffected
- Fixes the issue where some people didn't get bags if your account was created before 2/8/2026 at midnight. You should now get one when you log in
- Fixed the issue with the "limited" dye tub so that it now uses up the charges
- Limited dye tubs will show the hue number
