# SERVER PATCH 1.11.1

## May 14th, 2026

This is just a quick follow up patch to the big [1.11 patch](./patch-1.11.md)

## 🛠️ Bug Fixes & Enhancements

- Adjust the amount of gold and loot that drops during the DotW Gold Rush bonus (with further adjustments now able to be made in-game)
- Champ spawn are excluded from DotW bonuses
- The previous change to mining speed has been adjusted
- Created a gump for the `[dotw` command
- Added a new dungeon bonus, Boon of the Slayer, which increases your chances for a slayer weapon or instrument
- Fixed issue with dungeon milestones executing on non-active bonus dungeons
